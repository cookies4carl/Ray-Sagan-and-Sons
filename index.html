Ray Sagan & Sons has been the trusted name in industrial machinery moving across northern Illinois since 1967. What started with one straight truck and one small forklift has grown into a full-service machinery moving operation, but our family values have never changed.

Now in our third generation, we continue to bring the same dedication and expertise that built our reputation. Whether you need a single machine moved across town or a complete machine shop relocation, we handle every job with care and professionalism.

Our Services

From a single machine move to a complete facility relocation, we have the equipment and expertise to get the job done safely and on time.

Equipment Transport

We pick up, transport, and position industrial machinery directly into your shop area with care and precision.

Machine Shop Relocations

Complete machine shop relocations handled from start to finish, we manage the entire move so you can focus on your business.

In-Shop Repositioning

Need equipment rearranged within your facility? We handle in-shop repositioning quickly and safely.

Crating & Uncrating

We remove crating and shipping materials so you receive only your equipment, no disposal headaches.

Warehousing & Storage

Our 20,000 sq ft warehouse in Elk Grove Village offers secure temporary storage until you're ready for delivery.

Delivery & Placement

New or used industrial machines, we deliver and position equipment safely and efficiently wherever you need it.
